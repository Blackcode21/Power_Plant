Chainsaw Oils

Lubrita Chain Saw Oils are based upon high quality low aromatic solvent-refined base oils under addition of special additives to obtain the following properties: very high grade of tackiness, preventing the oil from fling of the chain, excellent lubricity. This chain-saw oils has been specially formulated to lubricate the chain of chain-saws preventing this oil from overheating and loss of strength. The high grade of tackiness prevents the oil to fling from the chain.
Note: The chain-saw oils can not be used for the lubrication of the engine of the saw!

For Biodegradable Chainsaw oils -Lubrita offer:
Lubrita Chain Saw Bio Oil 68 CEC-L-33-T-82 > 90% (3 weeks)
This oil based upon total biodegradable vegetable oils. Even at high temperatures this oil keeps a high grade of tackiness, preventing the oil to fling of the chain.

Lubrita Chain Saw Bio-Eco Oil 68
ECOLABEL, contains a large fraction of bio-based material. This is a chain-saw oil based upon total biodegradable natural ester oils under addition of special additives, gives reduced harm for water and soil during use.
